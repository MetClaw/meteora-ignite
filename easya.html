STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

EasyA Kickstart

Strategic counsel from Meteora's ecosystem team to a key partner.

The Vision: A place where builders anywhere in the world can access onchain capital, find a community of aligned speculators, and band together to bootstrap product growth and grow a genuine community. EasyA Kickstart should be the platform where that happens.

Not just "how to launch a token" — how to access global onchain capital, build a community financially invested in your success, and turn that into real product growth.

1. Discovery & Distribution

The current grid treats every project the same. Strong founders can't stand out and buyers can't filter signal from noise.

2. Graduation Path

What happens when a project gains traction? A graduation mechanism (like Believe's bonding curve → Meteora migration at $100K MC) gives founders a target and buyers confidence.

3. Categorization: Meme vs Startup

Separate memecoins from startup/utility tokens. Different categories attract different buyers with different expectations. Mixing them erodes trust.

4. Dedicated Telegram Per Project

Each project needs its own channel. Gives founders a place to engage holders and gives buyers a signal of team responsiveness.

5. Curation-First Community

Start with a curated Telegram — a small group with influence in ICM who believe in the Kickstart vision. Continuous feedback to Phil & Dom, advocacy for strong founders. Inner circle first, then open up. We did this for Believe (Believe Big Brains) and it drove staying power.

6. Legitimacy Badges

Hackathon winners should be prominently tagged. Colosseum or EasyA hackathon wins are trust signals — make them visible on the platform.

7. Builder Spotlight Templates

Recurring Builder Spotlights highlighting teams with verified track records. Content that draws attention to the platform while validating builders. Weekly on X, pinned in community channels.

8. X Spaces for Verified Builders

Give verified builders a stage. Validates the builder AND draws attention to Kickstart. Marketing and curation in one.

9. Platform Attention Strategy

Every recommendation above feeds into this. The explicit question: how does Kickstart get discovered as a launchpad, not just individual tokens?

10. Ongoing Trust Signalling

Trust compounds. Regular transparency updates, platform metrics (launches, volume, success stories), and visible commitment to quality over quantity.

11. Founder Credentials

Phil and Dom Kwok's personal track record is an underutilized asset. Cambridge, Wharton, Blackstone, Sullivan & Cromwell — plus $3B+ in projects from EasyA hackathons (including Cognition AI alumni). This credibility should be front and center in platform positioning, not buried.

12. The EasyA Narrative

Frame EasyA Kickstart as the greatest victory for onchain believers — a place where real builders meet real capital. Not just another launchpad, but the entry point for the next generation of crypto-native startups. The narrative should inspire builders to choose Kickstart over alternatives.

13. Scale to Thousands

The goal isn't dozens of launches — it's thousands. The platform, playbook, and community infrastructure should all be designed to scale.

14. Institutional Bridge

As RWA grows, Kickstart shouldn't just be for degens. Bridge serious builders from traditional finance and enterprise to onchain capital formation.

15. Global Access to Onchain Capital

Builders anywhere can access capital, find a community invested in their success, and bootstrap product growth. That's the thesis. Everything above serves it.

The Bigger Picture

The EasyA Arc: The same duo who brought blockchain to thousands are now unlocking capital raise opportunities for the most talented people of our generation. Education to capital formation. That's the story.
The Bedrock Convergence: Together with Bedrock (@BedrockFndn), we are raising the bar of what it means to raise onchain. Token holder protection. Clarity for subsequent investors. Legal infrastructure that makes internet capital markets legitimate.
The Mission: We are going to make internet capital markets genuinely open to all.

EasyA brings the builders. Bedrock brings the legal protection. Meteora brings the liquidity infrastructure. Together: open, protected, global internet capital markets.

Everything above — discovery, community, badges, spaces, categorization — is in service of this mission.
