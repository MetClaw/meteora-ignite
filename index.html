Get Started
01

PREPARE

Everything you need before launch day

1. Your One-Sentence Pitch

Short enough for an X bio. Clear enough to repeat from memory.

FORMULA
"[Project Name] — [what it does] for [who it's for]."

EXAMPLES

Umbra: "Incognito mode for Solana" — five words, instantly clear.
Solomon Labs: "Solana's stablecoin" — three words, unmistakable.

2. Show Conviction

Buy at least 10% of the supply (~3.5 SOL) when you create your coin. If you won't back your own idea, why should anyone else?

3. Set Up Your Surfaces

Every surface tells the same story. One idea, one token.

4. Build Your Launch Network

Line up supporters before anyone's watching.

MESSAGE TO YOUR NETWORK
"I'm launching [Project Name] on Solana on [date]. It's [one-sentence pitch]. Would love your support."

5. Prepare Your Content

Script your first three posts and record a short video. Don't improvise launch day.

1
The announcement: What you've launched, what it is, where to find it.
2
The story: Why you're building this. Your roadmap.
3
Proof of work: What you shipped today. Screenshot, demo, detail.
LAUNCH POST
Today I'm launching [Project Name] on Solana. [One sentence: what it does] I've been working on this since [context]. Here's where we're heading: [vision] CA: [address]
02

LAUNCH

Launch Mon–Thu, 10am–8pm ET

Tokenomics

Simple supply (1B or 1M). Publish allocations publicly. Community: 50-70%. Team: 10-20% (locked). Treasury: 10-20%.

Lock your team tokens. Streamflow or Bonfida. 6-month cliff minimum, 12-24 month vest. If team + insiders exceed 30%, your chart gets dumped.

Liquidity Setup

1
Pool type: Constant Product (simple) or DLMM on Meteora (concentrated, better efficiency, needs active management).
2
Initial liquidity: Minimum 10-20 SOL. Under 5 SOL looks like a rug.
3
Lock LP tokens: Burn or lock so you can't pull liquidity. #1 trust signal.
4
Verify on DexScreener: Token Verification Form + socials. 90% of buyers check here first.

04

NAVIGATE

What to know once you're live

Security & Rug-Proofing

Buyers check these in 10 seconds on DexScreener. If any are missing, smart money skips you.

Mistakes That Kill Launches

1
Selling your own supply early. It shows onchain. Trust dies permanently.
2
Going dark after launch. Two days of silence = community assumes you quit.
3
Promising prices. Talk about what you're building, not where the chart goes.
4
Paid KOLs before product. Mercenary capital dumps. Build first.
5
No face, no name. Anonymous founders get 10x less trust. Show up.

How Information Travels

Anything shared privately will travel publicly. If it's not ready for everyone, don't say it in DMs. Every holder should get info at the same time.
DM RESPONSE
"Everything I can share is on my X."
DM RESPONSE
"I'll share more when it's ready."

If Launch Is Flat

Not every launch pops immediately. The best response to a tough market is progress.

Double Down on Content

More posts, more engagement, more visibility.

Ship Something

Give people something new to talk about.

Don't Panic-Sell

Selling your own supply kills the project.
