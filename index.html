Get Started
01

PREPARE

Everything you need before launch day

1. Your One-Sentence Pitch

Short enough for an X bio. Clear enough to repeat from memory.

FORMULA
"[Project Name] — [what it does] for [who it's for]."

EXAMPLES

Umbra: "Incognito mode for Solana" — five words, instantly clear.
Solomon Labs: "Solana's stablecoin" — three words, unmistakable.

2. Show Conviction

Buy at least 10% of the supply (~3.5 SOL) when you create your coin. If you won't back your own idea, why should anyone else?

3. Set Up Your Surfaces

Every surface tells the same story. One idea, one token.

4. Build Your Launch Network

Line up supporters before anyone's watching.

MESSAGE TO YOUR NETWORK
"I'm launching [Project Name] on Solana on [date]. It's [one-sentence pitch]. Would love your support."

5. Prepare Your Content

Don't improvise launch day. Script your first three posts and record a short video.

1
The announcement: What you've launched, what it is, where to find it.
2
The story: Why you're building this. Your roadmap.
3
Proof of work: What you shipped today. Screenshot, demo, detail.
LAUNCH POST
Today I'm launching [Project Name] on Solana. [One sentence: what it does] I've been working on this since [context]. Here's where we're heading: [vision] CA: [address]

03

GROW

Give people reasons to talk about you

Post Templates

PROGRESS
Shipped [thing] today. Here's what it does.
DIRECTION
Our goal is to become [X]. Here's the plan.
MILESTONE
Hit [number] this week. Next up: [thing].
CLARITY
Someone asked [question]. Here's my answer.

Repeat yourself in different formats — video, graphics, threads, screenshots. Each reaches different people.

Content Rhythm

MondayWhat you're working on this week
WednesdayDeeper dive — decision, update, or lesson
FridayWhat you shipped, learned, what's next
MonthlyLonger reflection — what changed, where you're headed

Replies are weighted 27x more than likes on X. Engage in conversations — don't just post. Best times: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM EST on weekdays.

The Discovery Loop

1
Someone spots your project
2
They check your X, site, recent activity — under a minute
3
They form an opinion: "building something real" or "I'll check back later"
4
If impressed, they share it in a group chat or tweet
5
That person's network repeats the process

The 60-Second Window

15sRecent posts

10sSite or docs

Everything in this playbook serves that window.

Build a Core Team

5-20 people who understand your vision and can explain it to others. Your cohort, onchain communities (MonkeDAO, Superteam), and early holders who engage.

04

NAVIGATE

What to know once you're live

How Information Travels

Anything shared privately will travel publicly. If it's not ready for everyone, don't say it in DMs. Every holder should get info at the same time.
DM RESPONSE
"Everything I can share is on my X."
DM RESPONSE
"I'll share more when it's ready."

When Things Get Tough

Clear direction means your community debates the merits. Unclear direction means all the pressure falls on you.

Keep Communicating

Silence creates a vacuum. Updates build confidence.

Let Supporters Help

Your community will provide context if you've been consistent.

Ship Something

The best response to a tough market is progress.

If Launch Is Flat

Not every launch pops immediately. If it's quiet:

1
Double down on content — more posts, more engagement, more visibility
2
Run a community event — X Space, AMA, meme contest
3
Ship a feature or milestone — give people something new to talk about
4
Do NOT panic-sell your own supply — that kills the project
