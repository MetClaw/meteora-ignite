Onboard · welcome

You're joining an agent–human organisation.

This is unusual. Read this section first before doing anything else. The shape of the org will not match what you've seen elsewhere.

What MetEco is
Community + distribution as a service.
We package the crypto-native community playbook (drops, cohorts, tester networks, IRL rooms, content engines) and offer it to non-tokenized founders building real products. Frontier Founders is the flagship.
How it runs
9 agents + a few humans.
9 specialised Pentagon agents handle research, design support, compliance, builds, metrics, motion. Humans hold strategy, taste, relationships, taste calls, partnerships. You will route most work through agents.
Your job
Build conviction first. Ship second.
Day 1–2: no tasks. Just sit with Pentagon. Talk to every agent. Watch how the Director uses them. You cannot outsource your conviction on these tools — develop it directly.
What we don't do
No tokens. No memecoins.
No funding founders, no token launches, no investment advice. We compete on community + distribution craft, not on financial primitives.
Onboard · archetypes

Which one are you?

The org has three archetypes. No managers. No middleware. Self-locate before your first PRIME.

Archetype 01
DRI
Owns an outcome end-to-end. Decides what's good. Carries deliverables across the line. Briefs agents, integrates output, ships.
Archetype 02
IC / builder
Ships specific high-craft work. Yash (graphics), Tuan (multi-platform), MetClips team (clipping). Hands-on, opinionated, output-led.
Archetype 03
AI-founder
Sits with agents daily. Builds intuition. Sees workflows others miss because they're inside Pentagon every day. The Director is one. You should be too.

Onboard · how to brief an agent

Five rules. Use them every time.

Rule 01
Role-specific intake.
Don't ask agents "what's your status." Ask Orcus "what's your research queue?" Ask Atlas "what's your motion queue?" Generic questions get generic answers.
Rule 02
One P0 at a time.
Mapped to an outcome (founder growth · token volume · Meteora volume · weekly active LPs). P0 = unblocks others or has a deadline. Everything else is P1/P2.
Rule 03
Map dependencies first.
If agent B is blocked on agent A's output, A's task is P0 regardless of what's shinier. Sequence by who unblocks whom.
Rule 04
Review actual output.
Open the artifact. Read the copy. Look at the chart. Don't review the description an agent gives you of their own work. Trust nothing without looking.
Rule 05
When pausing, pause instantly.
If priorities shift mid-week, the fleet pauses immediately. No negotiation, no "but we should finish X." Re-prioritise in the next PRIME.

Thesis

The bet.

Why MetEco exists. Read this before anything else.

What works
The community layer of crypto.
NFTs, group chats, cohort mechanics, IRL rooms, incentive design. These tactics genuinely move people. They've just been trapped inside crypto-native projects.
What's commoditized
Building.
Anyone can ship an app now. AI collapsed the build moat. The hard part has shifted left: distribution, community, user acquisition.
The bet
Community + distribution as a service.
Take the crypto-native playbook — content, coordination, product testing, community building — and offer it as a service to promising founders. Prosumer Community Building & Product Distribution as a Service.
The flagship
Frontier Founders.
Designed for builders with non-tokenized projects (AI, robotics, hardware, frontier tech) who are open to using crypto tooling — drops, cohort mechanics, tester rewards — to grow their user base. Not for token launches. Not for memecoins. Real products, real users, real distribution.

Out of scope

No funding founders. No token launches. No investment advice. No tokenized projects in Frontier Founders. No anime IP. We do not compete with the memecoin / token-launch playbook — we offer the alternative.

Operating principles

How we work.

Seven tenets. Adapted from YC's AI-OS framework (Diana Hu). Every workflow, hire, and decision sits inside this frame.

01
AI is the operating system, not a tool.
Every workflow, decision, and process flows through an intelligent layer that's constantly learning. 9 Pentagon agents are the default operating layer. If a task can sit with an agent, it does.
02
Closed loops everywhere.
Every important process captures information, feeds it back into the system, and improves over time. Sun recap → Ariel gate → ship → Callisto metrics → next week's brief. Open loops are lossy. We don't run any.
03
The org is queryable.
The whole organisation should be legible to AI. Every important action produces an artifact. Notetakers in every meeting. Minimise DMs and emails. State lives in artifacts, not heads.
04
Software factories, not feature factories.
Humans write a spec and tests that define success. Agents generate the implementation and iterate until the tests pass. Galatea builds. The DRI defines what + judges output. Code is the agent's job.
05
No human middleware.
Velocity is only as fast as information flow. Every layer of human routing you remove is direct speed gain. No middle managers, no status rollups, no hand-offs that could be artifacts. Humans at the edges, agents in the middle.
06
Three archetypes. Working prototypes, not pitch decks.
IC / builder — everyone builds, everyone ships. Comes to meetings with working prototypes. DRI — one person, one outcome, no hiding. AI-founder — sits with agents daily, leads by example, never delegates the AI strategy.
07
Token-max, not headcount-max. Conviction is non-delegable.
Run an uncomfortably high API bill before adding humans. One person with AI tools = a preAI engineering team. Everyone on the team must sit with agents directly until they break their own priors about what's now possible. Day 1-2 = conviction only, no shipping.

Weekly Operating Rhythm (PRIME · PULSE · SHIP) Weekly · Fleet

The three-touch cadence that keeps the fleet aligned without daily standups. Three days do real work, the rest is heads-down execution.

Monday PRIME — set direction. Send each agent a role-specific intake question (not "what's your status"). Compile responses before assigning. Lock 1 P0 per agent, mapped to an outcome metric. Map dependencies (if B is blocked on A, A is P0). Push a single priority memo to the fleet before 10am.

Wednesday PULSE — catch slippage. Read reports (don't ask). Unblock anyone stuck. Catch trust-breaking details early (stale data labels, unverified claims, off-brand visuals). Escalate only what the fleet can't resolve internally.

Friday SHIP — close the week. Deliverables finalized or explicitly carried over. Callisto + Proteus deliver weekly numbers. One short summary memo to the Director: what shipped, what didn't, what changed strategically.

Why not daily: daily standups optimize for managers feeling in control. This rhythm optimizes for agents getting work done. Tue + Thu are protected execution days.

Bar: Skipping Monday means Tuesday assigns from assumption. Skipping Friday means next Monday starts cold. Don't.

Compliance Gate Continuous
Ariel checks: never-say, unverified claims, regulatory flags, dead platforms, brand consistency. Returns APPROVED or APPROVED + revisions. Async.
Builder Chat + Community Management Daily · Community

Owner: Community + Tester Lead. Channel: Meteora Ecosystem Telegram.

Daily duties:

  1. Post Spaces updates: pre-Space link, live link, recap link.
  2. Engage with builders: surface ships, answer questions, route to fleet.
  3. Field retweet + amplification requests. Triage: real ship → amplify, filler → decline politely.
  4. Surface launchpad/founder activity worth a spotlight slot.
  5. Flag friction signals (bugs, confusion, missing docs) to MetIgnite Lead + Orcus.

Bar: Presence is the product. Visible, responsive, useful. Not a megaphone.

Drop Calendar Quarterly
